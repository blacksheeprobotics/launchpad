About

Welcome to my corner of the web!

I’m passionate about robotics, artificial intelligence, and rocketry—pushing the boundaries of what’s possible and imagining the future. By day, I’m a developer and firmware engineer, turning ideas into tangible solutions and navigating the world of embedded systems. I’m also a proud father, balancing the joys of parenthood with a career in tech.

By night, I’m a maker at heart. You’ll often find me tinkering with new ideas, diving into hands-on projects, and exploring creative ways to bring concepts to life. Whether it’s designing the next robotics prototype, experimenting with AI algorithms, or dreaming up rocketry innovations, my mind is always buzzing with possibilities.

This space is where I share my journey—a blend of personal projects, professional insights, and the occasional musings of a curious mind. Thanks for stopping by, and feel free to connect if you’re as excited about the future as I am!
